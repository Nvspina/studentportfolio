About Me

I'm currently pursuing a graduate degree in Advanced Media Management at Syracuse University. My experience at Apple Retail instilled in me the value of crafting seamless, customer-first experiences while highlighting the impact of brand trust and innovation. At Syracuse, I’m building on that foundation through advanced coursework in artificial intelligence, marketing strategy, data analytics, and web development.

My path blends hands-on industry experience with academic depth, allowing me to approach consumer engagement from both strategic and operational perspectives. I’m especially drawn to how technology and data can foster deeper, more meaningful brand connections.

I’m always open to connecting with professionals in advertising, marketing, and tech who are passionate about creating data-informed, consumer-centric strategies.
